Human Capital Management

Recruitment for modern organisations has gone beyond the traditional HR functions of recruiting, training, retaining, policy development and firing. Our focus in human capital managment has shifted from the norm to investing in the sourcing of raw talent from the supply side (tertiary institutions and vocational schools), grooming them, and matching the refined talent in labour market to enable them meet up with demands of employers of labor.

Our efforts is not just aimed towards undergraduates or graduates but also we believe in “catching the senior secondary school leavers young” to help them discover their purpose and navigate their career path early enough.

No doubt, the rate of technology advancement and acceptability requires that a 21st century individual is proficient at the use of it. This has informed our use of world-class tools in the periodic assessment, qualification and training of human capital to help bring out the best in people.

Our services in this department is divided into the following areas:

1. CAREER PATHWAYS

This is a guided learning programme designed for young school leavers to build responsible and future-ready youths. This programme/process will provide them the required knowledge and skills to actively participate socially and economically to the growth of the country. The purpose takes the role of capacity building in contemporary in-demand skills to support their early employability and entrepreneurship.

2. PERSONAL TALENT MANAGEMENT

This is a course that seeks to complement the normal academic curriculum with the necessary competencies - knowledge, skills and attitudes - to operate successfully in the digital age and to seek defined opportunities and career paths in the world of work, entrepreneurship and academia. We add value to human capital through re-orientation, measurable workplace experience and acquisition of skills that will enable them become relevant in today’s labour market.

3. INTERNSHIP/ARTICLESHIP

We partner with the private sector and public institutions to take interns and corp members through our 3 months, 6 months or one year internship, intensive capacity development and experiential learning programmes aimed at prepping them for the future of work. These students will be sent on an advanced internship programme with various companies and organizations where they will utilize the knowledge.

4. MERCER/METTL ASSESSMENT TESTS

A global Leader in the Human Resource Consulting - Mercer Mettl has the mission is to enable organizations to make better people decisions across two key areas: acquisition and development. They have partnered with more than 4,000 corporates, 31 sector skills councils/government departments and 15+ educational institutions across more than 90 countries. We use the platform to deliver efficient, cost effective and technology-driven skill assessments that help our client organizations to build winning teams.
