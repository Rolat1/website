Technology and Innovation Solutions

Majority of businesses are faced with adapting to the evolution brought about by the prevalence of technology. This adaptation ranges from encouraging remote jobs to the need to digitally transform their operations. Integrating technology in business creates the process of automating and systematizing workflows and processes, keeps lines of communication open, enables more strategic planning and time management, among others. In essence, technology creates profitability and efficiency in businesses when employed right. As the first technology hub in Ogun State, Lead Resources supports corporate customers around the globe with IT-driven business solutions and helps our customers soar to greater heights riding on the shoulders of technology.

Our services in this department is divided into the following areas:

1. Technology for SDG Solutions/App Development - Technology can be a powerful tool in helping to solve the worldâ€™s most challenging issues including SDGs. But businesses often struggle to come up with a systemic way to strategically apply technology to their missions. Digital transformation is not simply about technology. Rather, it is fundamentally about connecting people, process, and technology to enable innovation and drive a step change in impact while helping fulfill SDG goals. This is why LEAD Resources supports organizations in ensuring their operations and missions align with certain SDG Goals with the aim of solving societal problems using technology.

2. Turning Academic Research to Innovation (TARTI) - It is general knowledge that in most tertiary institutions in Nigeria, after final-year students engage in project defense, their academic research papers are stacked and locked up in departmental resource centers for a few years after which they are disposed of. Hence, researches which should be adopted, funded and utilized for economic sustenance and national development are lost in the annals of history. The ripple effect of this course of action is the stagnation and retrogression of the nation and her citizens. In a bid to solve this problem, LEAD Resources created TARTI (Turning Academic Research to Innovation), an accelerator incubation programme designed to turn the solutions and recommendations embedded in the projects of young, talented Nigerians to innovative start-ups that can solve most of our pressing challenges. The programme involves the selection of innovative researches from students across various disciplines such as Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Agriculture, Statistics, etc. The innovators of these researches are made to undergo incubation until their ideas are developed and eventually scaled for maximum profit for themselves and the nation at large.

4. Incubation and Acceleration -Our incubation and acceleration is an Entrepreneurship Training and Innovation support programme that involves an extended 6 months programme called Start-Up Loop which is designed to take Start-up Entrepreneurs through a tested and proven process that will help them articulate, build and convert their viable business idea into innovative and marketable products and services. The initiative will develop the capacities of the entrepreneurs by equipping them with the necessary tools, skills and techniques through effective experiential training, pitching events and supportive coaching. We also assist in linking them up with potential investors.

5. Founding Member, Ogun State Digital Economy Advisory Group (DEAG) -The DEAG is a body of key stakeholders in the Information Technology ecosystem of Ogun State of which the Director of the Ogun State Bureau of Information Technology (BIT) is a member. The DEAG in its analysis of the economic environment chose to pivot the development of the digital economic ecosystem of Ogun State on six Economic pillars namely: Digital Government, Digital Business, Digital Infrastructure, Innovation Driven Entrepreneurship, Human Capital and Socio-Cultural. The facilitation of industrial evolution of a State cannot be done at a sustainably incremental rate using analogue processes, operations, administration and financing models. Hence, the deliberate effort by DEAG to strategically drive the economic growth desired by the citizens of the State through a strategic plan hinged on digital pillars which is also in line with the National Digital Economy Policy and Strategy (2020-2030) of NITDA.

6. HAMORAH - It is the pilot project of the Ogun State Digital Ecosystem Advisory Group (DEAG) - a 100% private sector-led think-tank with the mandate of facilitating the digital development of the economy of Ogun State. Hamorah is a technology platform focused on creating, identifying, and promoting the socio-cultural heritage, and activities of Ogun State. It is to create and establish a competitive framework for the Indigenes of the State also to provide indigenous contents in areas of people, artifacts, places, festivals, events, commerce, history and a lot more. The platform provides access to knowledge about different traditions and cultures but also the opportunity to contribute to the platform by posting their own content and engaging with that of others.

7. SDG Solutions Convergence -an initiative that creates a process and environment for brilliant minds to transform ideas into innovations and inventions. This is targeted towards individuals, entrepreneurs and organizations to stimulate commitment and action towards creating a technology driven Solution in-line with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The Convergence consists of a holistic awareness on how to identify the problems by looking at the Sustainable Development Goals, determining and creating solutions leveraging on the use of Emerging Technology and Design Thinking, and then finally turning these solutions/innovations into business opportunities using Business Model Canvas/LEAN Canvas. Also, we belong to a State consortium saddled with the responsibility of SDG mainstreaming into enterprise and business processes, as well as advocacy for solutions thinking and engagements.
