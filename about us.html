About us

WHO WE ARE

Lead Resources is a strategic human capital development social enterprise with a focus on governance, enterprise and technology hinged on generating opportunities, stimulating innovation and creating solutions to address challenges in our polity.

We serve as a pre-incubation space for technologists, social and business entrepreneurs and impact investors to co-found new solutions.

OUR MANDATE

MISSION

We support people, organizations and governments to be the best they can be by using principles, practices, tools and techniques that are world-class.

VISION

To be the indisputable knowledge leadership and real solution provider out of Africa.

OUR VALUES

The service we render will be guided by our beliefs and commitment to support people, organizations and governments to be the best they can be by using principles, practices, tools and techniques that are world class.
