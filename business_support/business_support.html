Business Support

We are very much aware of how the internal processes of a business can be time-consuming thereby reducing the performance of the business if not handled efficiently. Considering the major areas that most business owners are having difficulty in, we offer six (6) major Business Development Support Services to organizations. This Support includes a range of services that your organization will need for more visibility, growth and profitability.

We have a certified team of experts that would help your business, and organization with these services.

DATA ANALYSIS

From customer data to sales records, your business probably generates tons of raw data that don’t seem to make any sense. However, in this digital age, data is the new gold which if properly mined, cleaned, analysed and visualized will help gain deep insights about your business and use same to make fact-based and wise desicions. We can support you to collect, collate and analyze your data sets with the aim of tracking and predicting the growth of your business.

CONTENT CREATION/RESEARCH

This service is to analyze your business needs, and develop a unique strategy based on that information that will benefit your company's long-term growth and brand visibility. This varies from website content and blog posts, to papers, communiqué, company policies, and repertoires.

WEB & SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Every organization needs an online presence. One way to do this is to have a website with detailed information about your organization or an application that makes digitizes your services, increases productivity and adds to your bottomline. For this reason, this service includes a range of solutions from a team of Web Developers who will create and maintain your website to a suite of corporate pieces of software and collaboration tools that can be customized to your organization’s needs. We can also onboard and train your staff to operate these pieces of software.

DIGITAL MARKETING

Speaking of online presence, beyond web development, we have digital marketers to help you use social media platforms to drive awareness and visibility for your brand. These platforms include Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, the company's website, Google my Business, search engine optimization (SEO) rankings, email marketing, display advertising, and the company's owned blog where necessary.

UI/UX & GRAPHICS DESIGN

This service involves our front-end web developers responsible for applying interactive, and visual design on your website, and applications for a seamless and swift user experience. The graphics design team works hand in gloves with the digital marketing team to produce the creatives needed to amplify your business to your customers.

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

We have professional Accountants who will work closely with your in-house sales personnel or book-keeper to ensure all your financial transactions are properly recorded and digitally kept via cutting edge accounting softwares. We can make you investor ready and revenue regulator compliant by handling all your accounting and tax needs. If you subscribe to our financial management services, we are going to offer you timely and relevant financial advice that will put you way ahead of your competitors.
