Capital Project Management

Capital projects are projects that help maintain or improve an organisation's assets. Project management is at the core of what we do as a company. Under this service, we are in charge of projects in a wide variety of industries including but not limited to real estate development, construction, agriculture, power engineering, technology, human capacity management and development programmes and lots more.

Our capital project management department is responsible for sourcing, initializing, executing, monitoring and reporting on both internal and external capital projects carried out by the organisation on behalf of our clients and partners. We use various types of project management process to meet specific needs of certain projects which includes:

WATERFALL PROJECT MANAGEMENT
AGILE PROJECT MANAGEMENT
LEARN PROJECT MANAGEMENT
